Mie Ayam

Description

Mie Ayam, which is a noodle dish with chicken and various toppings, is one of the most sought-after street foods in Indonesia.

The word Mie Ayam literally means "chicken noodle" in English. It is a dish made of egg noodles, spiced shredded chicken, mushrooms, and a sweet-savory sauce. It is often served with soy sauce, chili sauce, choy sum, and chicken broth.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs with skin and bone
  • Egg noodles
  • Mushrooms
  • Garlic
  • Cooking oil
  • Scallion
  • Ground white pepper
  • Turmeric powder
  • Ground coriander
  • Shallots
  • Candlenuts
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Pak choi, bok choi or choy sum
  • Chili sauce
  • Cucumber salad
  • Crispy fried shallots/onions

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken by taking off the skin off and cutting it into small pieces.
  2. Make chicken spiced oil by frying the skin in cooking oil until it's slightly brown and crispy.
  3. Add chopped garlic, and wait till the color turns golden brown and the oil is fragrant. Then the chicken spiced oil is ready.
  4. Boil water and add the chicken, ginger garlic, scallion, ground white pepper, and salt.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, take it off and cut it into the shape of dice or shred it.
  6. Put the bone back into the soup and let it simmer while you work on other items.
  7. Make a spice paste of shallots, garlic, and candlenuts. You can use a pestle, mortar, or food chopper.
  8. Fry the spice paste and add the turmeric powder and ground coriander. When you can smell the aroma, add the chopped chicken and mushrooms. Let it cook until the liquid evaporates.
  9. Once the chicken topping is done, blanch the pak choi, bok choi, or choy sum. Then, get your noodles ready.
  10. Drain the cooked noodles and pour it into your serving bowl.
  11. Add oyster sauce, sweet soy sauce, and chicken oil. Then stir it till all of them mixed.
